Postgraduate Admission:


  • Admission toM.Tech programme with two year duration are usually performed in April-May months of every year.
  • Details about eligibility criteria with regards to academic qualifications, experience, application forms, online application procedure, selection procedure etc. are advertised on the Institute website.
  • Admission to Ph.D programme is conducted twice in an year, once in April-May time frame and the other in the month of December.
  • Eligibility for admission in Ph.D courses :
    • Usually, persons holding Masters degree in engineering and technology are encouraged to apply for Ph.D.
    • Persons with Bachelors degree in engineering and technology or Masters degree in Science(subject to some restrictions as prescribed) with exceptional academic records may also be considered for admissions.
      • =======
      • Admission to Two-Year M.Tech Programmes is made on the basis of GATE Score. In addition, there is provision of admitting sponsored candidates from academic institutions, industries and R&D organizations.
      • Admission to Ph.D. programmes is made on the basis of GATE/NET scores and through sponsorship by the employer.
      • Foreign nationals residing in India or abroad are admitted only to the post graduate and Ph.D. programmes under:
        • self-financing scheme, and
        • cultural exchange fellowship programme.
