Admission Procedure:

Undergraduate Admission:


  • Admission to Four-Year B.Tech. programme and Five-Year Dual Degree Programme is made through the Joint Entrance Examination(JEE) conducted jointly by the IITs, Indian Institute of Technology (Banaras Hindu University) and the Indian School of Mines University, Dhanbad.

Postgraduate Admission:


  • Admission toM.Tech programme with two year duration are usually performed in April-May months of every year.
  • Details about eligibility criteria with regards to academic qualifications, experience, application forms, online application procedure, selection procedure etc. are advertised on the Institute website.
  • Admission to Ph.D programme is conducted twice in an year, once in April-May time frame and the other in the month of December.
  • Eligibility for admission in Ph.D courses :
    • Usually, persons holding Masters degree in engineering and technology are encouraged to apply for Ph.D.
    • Persons with Bachelors degree in engineering and technology or Masters degree in Science(subject to some restrictions as prescribed) with exceptional academic records may also be considered for admissions.
  • More details about eligibility of other degrees, eligibility for funding support and selection procedure can be found in Institute website.
  • Ceramic Department has the ambition of increasing both the quality and quantity of Ph.D level research.
  • If you are intrested in doing cutting edge research in any area of ceramic engineering and have an outstanding academic record, do write to the Head of Department or any other other faculty member with matching research interests about your interest to join thePh.D programme of the Department.
